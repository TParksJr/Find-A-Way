Who Are We?

 

We are proud to share this application that will help you to decide a cost efficient way to reach your destination i.e. by driving yourself or taking an uber.

We will display the cost of driving i.e. parking and gas, and taking an uber.

How it works?

 

To provide you with the results, all we need is your current location and destination. You can also add your leaving time and number of people for more accuracy.

Once, all the inputs are given, the application will calculate the costs and show you the average prices side by side in order for you to compare and decide.

Who are our audience?

 

This conceptualization allows for a broad swath of potential users. Aside from users interested in not spending money unnecessarily, this application can fulfill a few other needs.

For example, those with mobility issues, senior citizens, expecting mothers, or parents/guardians on an outing with children might prefer to take an uber if the nearest parking is farther than is feasible or reasonably convenient for them.
